Project Name: “D.E.A.D. (Drugs End All Dreams)"


“D.E.A.D. (Drugs End All Dreams)" aimed to educate students on the detrimental effects of drug usage, fostering a shift in attitudes towards substance abuse. Collaborating with the Moratuwa Police and school officials, our project emphasized the long-term consequences of drug use, highlighting alternative methods for a fulfilling life. Through awareness programs and group activities, we successfully conveyed the message of drug prevention and rehabilitation to students. Led by Project Chairman Leo Nipuni Wijesekara, our initiative focused on empowering youth to make informed choices and safeguard their futures. This project was awarded as the 2 nd Runner-Up for the “Best Project for Drug Prevention and Rehabilitation” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2.
