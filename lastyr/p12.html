Project Name: Apekshawata Apekshawak


The community project "Apekshawata Apekshawak" was launched to address the critical shortage of medications for pediatric cancer patients at Apeksha Hospital, Maharagama, amidst the country's economic crisis. By fundraising for essential medicine donations and raising awareness through flyers and videos, we aimed to alleviate the suffering of vulnerable children and prevent potential medical disasters. Led by Project Chairman Leo Ranudika Dinenthi, our initiative received positive feedback from hospital staff, marking the successful first phase of our project. Falling under the categories of "Pediatric Cancer" and "Disaster Management and Prevention," our efforts strive to provide timely support and spotlight the urgent needs of pediatric patients. This Project was awarded as the winner of the category “Best Project for Pediatric Cancer” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2.
