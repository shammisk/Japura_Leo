Project Name: Diabet Care


The project "Diabet Care" aimed to raise awareness about diabetes and provide screening for non-academic staff at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Organized by the Leo Club, the free testing camp targeted undergraduates and laborers. Led by Project Chairmen Leo Shamindya Rupasinghe and Leo Himashi Lokuge, the initiative received positive feedback, with over 120 tests conducted. By prioritizing the well-being of members and supporting staff, we strive to continue organizing health-oriented programs in the future and this project was awarded as the first Runner for the category “Best Project for Diabetes” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2.
