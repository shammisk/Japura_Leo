Project Name: Save Her


"Save Her" was a transformative project dedicated to empowering young girls with the knowledge and skills to prevent instances of sexual harassment. Led by Project Chairman Leo Sonali Abeysinghe, our initiative focused on raising community awareness and providing support for victims of sexual harassment. Through a multi-phase approach including online articles, individual videos, and physical events, we aimed to educate and empower women to courageously stand up against harassment. By addressing this critical issue, we strive to create a safer and more equitable society for women and our younger generation. "Save Her" falls under the category of "Women Empowerment," emphasizing our commitment to supporting and uplifting women in our community.
