Project Name: Stay Alert


The Project Stay Alert was a comprehensive initiative targeting Crime and Accident Prevention, as well as Disaster Management and Prevention, which was led by Leo Nipuni Wijesekara. In its inaugural phase, an impactful awareness session centered on crime prevention was hosted. Following this, the second phase saw the implementation of road signs in critical accident-prone zones across the Uva province, emphasizing accident prevention. Proud winners of the award of “Best Project for Crime and Accident Prevention”, and the 1 st Runner up for “Best Project for Disaster Management and Prevention”, during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2 this initiative aimed to safeguard communities and enhance safety measures.
