Project Name: Sihinayata Sihinayak


The project Sihinayata Sihinayak was a heartfelt service project aimed at supporting school children under 16 years who had been affected by the economic crisis in the Mathugama area. This was led by the Project Chairman Leo Hiruni Ishanka. Being awarded as the 2 nd runner up for the awards “Best Project for Spotlight on Children” and “Best Project for Quality Education and Literacy” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2, this initiative sought to provide essential stationery items to these deserving children, making a positive impact on their lives.
