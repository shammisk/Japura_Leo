Project Name: Save the Seaside


"Save the Seaside" was a monthly beach cleanup series spanning across the island, dedicated to environmental preservation. In collaboration with municipal councils, this project focused on thorough beach cleaning, recycling essential plastics and debris, and fostering a lasting positive impact on the environment. Not only was this project committed to responsible consumption and waste management, but it also aimed to support livelihoods by repurposing the plastics that were collected. Awarded as the 1 st Runner Up for the “Most Outstanding Continuous Project”, during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2, this initiative aimed to promote Responsible Consumption & Waste Management" and was led by Project Chairman Leo Melissa Handapangoda.
