Project Name: Husmata Husmak


The project "Husmata Husmak," a transformative endeavor dedicated to environmental sustainability and human well-being, marked a milestone on April 28, 2023. Leo Sandaruwan Sooriyabandara, the District Contest Director, was invited as the honored Chief Guest for the second phase of this project. Spearheaded by Project Chairman Leo Hithmi Pankaja, their efforts strived to foster a healthier planet and community. Proud winners of the “Best Project for Responsible Consumption and Waste Management”, during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2, this project had a significant impact on environmental sustainability and human well-being.
