Project Name: Diviyata Aruthak


The project, "Diviyata Aruthak" led by Leo Ovindu Dilanka marked an initial stride towards spotlighting children's mental health and well-being. The inaugural visit was to Piliyandala Delthara Primary School which targeted students from grades 8 to 11, along with parents of younger children. Dr. Bhathiya Dasanayake, a distinguished psychiatrist and early childhood development specialist, was selected as our resource person. With a focus on nurturing mental wellness and providing invaluable support and guidance to children and families alike, this project was able to win the award for “Best Project for Mental Health and Well-being” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2.
