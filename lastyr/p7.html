Project Name: Art of Living


"Art of Living" was a mental health awareness initiative tailored for students at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Our project which focused on improving Mental Health & well-being along with Quality Education & Literacy of students, began with selecting a resource person proficient in psychiatry and early childhood development. Under the guidance of this expert, we were able to foster a culture of holistic well-being and promote mental wellness among university students.
