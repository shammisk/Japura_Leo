Project Name: Maathru Arunalla


Amidst the economic challenges Sri Lanka faced in recent years, we initiated “Maathru Arunalla" a series of projects, which focused on pregnant mothers from low-income families in the Horana area. Our dedicated efforts targeted this vulnerable group, providing essential items such as pampers, nets, baby napkins, and other necessities for newborns, along with nutritional support for the mothers. This project was led by Project Chairman Leo Oshadi Dilthara and it aimed to alleviate the burdens faced by these families and promote maternal and child wellbeing. This project was able to secure the winning award for two categories, “Best Project for Health, Nutrition and Food Safety” and “Best Project for Poverty and Better Life” during the Insperenzo Awards; 18th Annual District Conference of Leo District 306 C2.
