Project Name: Happy Cure - Phase 01


The "Happy Cure" project, conducted at The Salvation Army's Child Development Center in Rajagiriya, successfully promoted the holistic well-being of children under 16. The project, guided by two medical students, examined children's dental and eye conditions, referred those in need for treatment, and focused on nurturing their educational aspirations. The team engaged with the children, identifying areas needing support and inspiring them to pursue their goals. The project highlighted the interconnectedness of health and education, and the lessons learned from it can inspire future efforts to nurture children's physical and educational needs.
