Project Name: Accolades of Success 3.0


In a grand celebration of dedication, service, and excellence, the Leo Club of District 306 C2 University of Jayewardenepura proudly presented a comprehensive showcase of their outstanding awards, and achievements at a special Vice-Chancellor meeting at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura on the 11th of August 2023. The event served as a platform to highlight the club’s remarkable journey, its impact on the university community, and its commitment to making a positive change for those in need. Led by a dynamic group of young leaders, the Leo Club of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura 306 C2 has continuously demonstrated its unwavering dedication to service and community engagement. The club’s diverse range of achievements not only underscores its commitment to social betterment but also stands as a testament to its ability to drive meaningful change.
