Project Name: Enviro Volt


To promote sustainability and empower the community the Leos of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura made a significant contribution to the Parama Dhamma Nivesa Pirivena Temple, Borelesgamuwa by donating a Solar Panel and a generator. These solar panels were given to us by the Leo Club of Royal College. The objective of this endeavor was to equip the temple with sustainable energy methods, thereby minimizing its carbon footprint and cutting down on electricity expenses. We wanted to encourage environmental responsibility in the community and support the temple's sustainability goals with this donation. The accomplishment of this donation not only strengthens the temple's energy resilience but also shows how dedicated our Leos are to environmental stewardship and community service.
