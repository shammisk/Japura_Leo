Project Name: Pivithuru Siripa


On January 31st, 2024, 16 individuals participated in a cleanup initiative at Sri Pada, focusing on environmental conservation and preserving the sacred site's beauty. The group sorted collected trash into recyclables and non-recyclables, setting aside plastic bottles, cans, and recycling paper. Other waste was disposed of responsibly. The efforts not only removed litter but also minimized environmental impact. Visitors were drawn to the cleanup, inspiring others to join. The team demonstrated the effectiveness of collective efforts and established a standard for conscientious environmental protection and waste management.
