Project Name: Sanhinda


The සංහිඳ (Sanhinda) project, a compassionate initiative, provided essential support to those in need at the Moratuwa Pathum Uyana Mother's Charitable Foundation. The team distributed dry foods, contributed to meals, and provided educational materials, including A4 sheets and clothes for handcrafts. A music event was held to create a positive atmosphere and foster a sense of belonging. The project also provided socialization, community building, and selfexpression for those with limited verbal skills. The project aimed to make a tangible impact on the Moratuwa community.
