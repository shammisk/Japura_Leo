Project Name: Sith Arana


On January 31, 2024, we provided dinner and essential materials to "IBITAD Grand Smile, Care of Elders" in Haldumulla. The project aimed to improve the well-being and dignity of elderly residents by providing them with home-dinner meals, dry rations, bedsheets, and medicines. This not only ensures their comfort and health but also provides a sense of security and wellbeing. Medication is crucial for managing chronic conditions like diabetes, heart disease, hypertension, and arthritis, which can lower medical costs and improve overall quality of life. We organized a special dinner event, serving delicious, nutritious meals and providing essential materials to the elderly residents, aiming to lift their spirits and foster community belonging.
